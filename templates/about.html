What BLMS does?

Academic monitoring, attendance automation, and school reporting in one place

AI

Attendance

Face recognition and teacher tools record present, late, very late, and absent attendance in real time.

GR

Performance Analytics

Track marks with visual graphs, subject trends, and performance insights for each student.

Rs

Fees

Track paid and pending fees, create reminders, and keep payment history visible for staff and students.

RC

Recommendations

Generate data-driven recommendations from attendance, marks, and fee records to support improvement.

RP

Dashboard And Reports

Use centralized dashboards and downloadable reports to review attendance, results, and fee status quickly.

RL

Secure Access

Students and teachers have separate role-based access, with safer password storage and controlled views.
